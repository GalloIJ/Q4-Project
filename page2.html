CHARACTERS IN NOLI ME TANGERE
Juan Crisostomo Ibarra
  • Juan Crisostomo Ibarra is the protagonist of the novel. He is a young Filipino who returns to the Philippines after studying in Europe.
    		•
    Maria Clara de los Santos
  • Maria Clara is the daughter of Captain Tiago and is known for her beauty and grace. She is the love interest of Ibarra.
    		•
    Elias
  • Elias is a mysterious and strong young man who becomes an important figure in Ibarra's life and in the fight against injustice.
    		•
    Padre Damaso
  • Padre Damaso is a corrupt and abusive friar who wields power in the town and is a source of conflict in the story.
    		•
    Sisa
  • Sisa is a poor and mentally unstable woman who suffers greatly and is the mother of two main characters in the story.
